
**Form Feminine by adding suffixes to noun:
-ā, -ī, -ānī, -inī, -unī, -aka>-ikā**
### Nominative
>[!important] -s -(y)o

| stem | sl. | pl.    |
| ---- | --- | ------ |
| ā    | ā   | ā, āyo |
| i    | i   | ī, īyo |
| ī    | ī   | pe.     |
| u    | u   | ū, uyo |
| ū    | ū   | pe.       |

### Vocative
> similar to nom. only singular ī, ū change to short i, u

### Accusative
>plural keep the same
>singular: (short)ṃ

| stem | sl. | pl.    |
| ---- | --- | ------ |

| ī    | iṃ   | pe.     |
